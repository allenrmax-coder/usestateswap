Student-built marketplace for campus life

Buy smarter. Sell easier. Swap and connect.

StateSwap is a peer-to-peer marketplace app for students, by students. We are launching with a Penn State-centered community in mind, while making it clear that we are not officially affiliated with Penn State at this stage.

The goal is to give students one cleaner, safer place to list items, find campus deals, discover student-run services, and build more direct peer-to-peer connections.
