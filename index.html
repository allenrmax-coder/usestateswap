StateSwap is a peer-to-peer student marketplace built to help Penn State students buy, sell, swap, and promote services in one cleaner, more trusted place.

We’re starting with a Penn State-focused community and early entrepreneurship program. StateSwap is student-driven, but not officially affiliated with Penn State.
